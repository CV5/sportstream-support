Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SportStream ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that allows users to broadcast live sports games to social media platforms while displaying a real-time scoreboard overlay. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information we collect

When you log in with Facebook, we collect your name, profile picture, and Facebook User ID. We also collect content you create within the app, including game scores, player statistics, team names, and live stream metadata (title, date, sport type).

We do not collect payment information, precise location data, or any sensitive personal information.

How we use your information

We use your Facebook account to authenticate your identity and to publish live video streams on your behalf, using only the permissions you explicitly grant. Score data and game statistics are stored in our database to enable real-time display during streams and to show your game history on your profile.

Live streaming

When you choose to go live, SportStream accesses your device camera and microphone and transmits the video feed directly to Facebook's servers using the RTMP protocol. SportStream does not record or store your video content. Your Facebook access token is used solely to create and manage live video sessions on your behalf via the Facebook Graph API.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Game scores and statistics you mark as public are visible to other SportStream users. Your Facebook profile information is used only within the app and is never shared externally.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete your SportStream account at any time from the app settings. Upon deletion, all your game data and profile information is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

To revoke SportStream's access to your Facebook account independently, visit Facebook Settings → Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites and remove SportStream from the list.

Camera and microphone

SportStream requests access to your device camera and microphone exclusively to enable live video streaming. This access is only active while you have an active stream running. We do not access your camera or microphone at any other time.

Children's privacy

SportStream is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: privacy@sportstream.app
